1. What Is This System?

WoodWings PMS is the company's project management system. It tracks every project from client enquiry through design, production, installation, and final handover.

As the Manager, you have the most operational visibility in the system. Your responsibilities include:

  • Approving or rejecting tasks submitted by the SED, Fabrication, and Installation teams
  • Coordinating deliveries and production timelines (F2, F3, F6)
  • Assigning installation teams and managing gate passes
  • Tracking payments and monitoring project progress
  • Recording timesheets for workers

You have visibility across all departments and all projects, making you the operational hub of the system.

2. How to Log In

  1. Open your web browser and navigate to the WoodWings app URL.
  2. Enter your email address and password.

Your Approval Role

The most critical part of your role in the system is approving tasks. When a team member (SED, Fabrication, or Installation) completes a gated task, it moves to Pending Approval and appears in your Tasks tab.

  1. Open the pending task. Review the work — check the attached documents, dates, notes, and any comments.
  2. If the work is satisfactory, click Approve. The task becomes Completed and the next task in the chain unlocks.
  3. If something needs correction, click Reject. Add a comment explaining what needs to change. The task is sent back to the team member as To Do.
Important: When you reject a task, always write a clear comment. The team member will only see your comment — they won't know why it was rejected otherwise.

5. How to Work a Task

  1. Go to the Tasks tab and find the task (or use the Approvals section for pending items).
  2. Click the task card to open it.
  3. Review the content. For approval tasks: read any attachments, check dates, verify the work is complete.
  4. For your own tasks (not approvals): change status to In Progress, fill required fields, then set to Completed.
  5. For approval tasks: click Approve or Reject (with a comment if rejecting).

6. Special Task Types

Manager Approval Flow

Most gated tasks from SED, Fabrication, and Installation land in your Pending Approval queue. The process is always the same:

  1. Open the task from your Tasks tab (look for Pending Approval status).
  2. Review all attached documents, dates, and notes provided by the team.
  3. Click Approve if everything is in order. The next task unlocks automatically.
  4. Click Reject if changes are needed. Write a clear comment. The task returns to the submitter.

F3 — Material Order

F3 is the material ordering form. It is triggered either by SED (for samples) or by the fabrication team when they need materials to start production.

  1. A task named "F3 — Order Materials" will appear in your task queue.
  2. Open it. You will see a form with a material table: each row has Material, Quantity, Unit, Supplier, and Notes.
  3. Review the request. You can add rows if needed using the + Add Row button.
  4. There are two order types you can select: Small Order (quick, local purchase) or Big Order (formal supplier PO).
  5. Submit the order. The fabrication team can see that materials are on the way.
📸

[SCREENSHOT: F3 Order panel — Small/Big order toggle, material table with rows, + Add Row and Submit buttons]

F6 — Handover Sheet

The F6 Handover Sheet is filled when installation is complete and the project is being formally handed over to the client.

  1. A task named "F6 — Handover Sheet" will appear.
  2. Open it. Fill in the handover details: completion date, client sign-off notes, outstanding items if any.
  3. Attach the signed handover document (photograph or PDF).
  4. Submit. The project stage advances toward Closing.

Gate Pass

A gate pass authorises the movement of materials and workers to a project site. You create gate passes for delivery and installation trips.

Gate passes have four sections to fill:

  1. Vehicle Details — vehicle type, plate number, driver name
  2. Workers — list of workers going on this trip
  3. Materials — what is being transported (items, quantities)
  4. Site Details — destination address, site contact name
  1. Go to the Forms page (sidebar) or open it from a relevant task.
  2. In the Gate Passes section, click + New Gate Pass.
  3. Fill in all four sections of the form.
  4. Click the Print icon (always visible on every gate pass card) to print the physical document for the driver.
  5. When the delivery is confirmed, click Mark Delivered on the gate pass card.
  6. To cancel a gate pass, click Cancel on the card.
📸

[SCREENSHOT: Gate Pass card on the Forms page — showing print icon, Mark Delivered button, and Cancel button]

Assign Installation Team

When a project is ready for installation, you must assign the installation team.

  1. A task called "Choose Installation Team" will appear in your task queue.
  2. Open it. Select the team members who will work on this installation from the worker list.
  3. Set the planned installation start date.
  4. Add any special notes or instructions for the team in the assignment notes field.
  5. Submit. The installation team will see the assignment note in their dashboard.

F2 — Production Timeline

The F2 form sets the production schedule for a specific project item — when fabrication should start and when it is expected to be complete.

  1. A task named "F2 — Production Timeline" appears when an item is ready to go to fabrication.
  2. Open it. Fill in Planned Production Start Date and Expected Fabrication End Date.
  3. Submit. Fabrication can now see their schedule for this item.

7. Notifications

You receive notifications when tasks are submitted for your approval, when SED or fabrication teams complete key steps, and for weekly reminders.

Click the bell icon in the sidebar to open the Notifications page.

📸

[SCREENSHOT: Notifications page — unread notifications with blue dot, All/Unread toggle, Mark all read button]

  • Unread notifications show a blue dot and blue background highlight.
  • Click any notification to mark it as read and navigate to the relevant task.
  • Mark all read button (top right) clears all at once.
  • The Live Alerts section at the top of the page shows critical items: pending approvals, stale projects, overdue payments.
  • A weekly reminder is sent every Friday morning automatically.
  • A monthly audit reminder is sent on the 1st of each month.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which tasks need my approval right now?

Go to your Tasks tab and look for cards with the Pending Approval status. These are submitted by team members and are waiting for your review. The Live Alerts section on the Notifications page also surfaces urgent pending approvals.

I rejected a task — why can't I see it in my queue anymore?

When you reject a task, it is returned to the original submitter as To Do. It disappears from your approval queue until they resubmit it. Once they resubmit, it will reappear in your queue as Pending Approval again.

A project seems stuck — how do I find out why?

Go to the Projects tab and find the project. Click it to see all its tasks and their statuses. A project usually gets stuck because a task is in Pending Approval and hasn't been reviewed yet, or because a task is Locked waiting for something else to complete.

How do I print a gate pass?

Go to the Forms page from the sidebar. In the Gate Passes section, find the gate pass you want to print. Click the print icon (always visible on every gate pass card). Your browser's print dialog will open.

What is the difference between a Small Order and a Big Order in F3?

A Small Order is a direct local purchase — the materials are bought immediately from a nearby supplier without a formal purchase order. A Big Order is a formal supplier order, usually for larger quantities or specific materials that need to be requested through official channels.

Can I change a task status for someone from another team?

Your role allows you to edit manager-specific fields on any task (like approval status, completion date, priority flag, and start date). You cannot change a fabrication team member's fabrication-specific fields. If you need to override something unusual, ask the superadmin.

I need to track a payment — where do I go?

Go to the Payments tab on your dashboard. All projects with active payment schedules are listed here. You can record payment receipts and view the full payment history for each project.

How do I manage worker timesheets?

Go to the Timesheets tab. This shows daily attendance logs submitted by the installation team. You can review them, add comments, and generate summaries for payroll purposes.
