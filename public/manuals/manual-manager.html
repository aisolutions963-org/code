Manager Role Guide

Operations management. Approvals, project oversight, payments, installation team coordination, delivery scheduling, and daily worker timesheets — all in one place.

Section 1

Introduction

As a Manager in WoodWings PMS, you are the operational backbone of the company. Projects cannot advance through key milestones without your review and approval.

Your Role: Manager

Your responsibilities in the system:

  • Reviewing and approving or rejecting tasks submitted for approval by SED, Fabrication, and Installation teams
  • Creating Gate Passes and scheduling delivery dates
  • Assigning installation team members to projects
  • Overseeing payment records and the payment calendar
  • Logging and reviewing daily worker timesheets
  • Submitting F3 material orders, F5 quotation items, and F6 Handover forms when needed
  • Monitoring all active projects, deliveries, and materials

How to Log In

1
Navigate to the login pageOpen the application in your browser and go to /login.
2
Enter your email and passwordUse your assigned credentials.
3
You are redirected to your dashboardThe system sends you to /dashboard/mgr automatically.

To log out, click the menu at the bottom of the left sidebar and select Log Out.

Section 3

Task Management & Approvals

The most critical function of the Manager role. When a team member submits a task for approval, only you can unblock it.

Task Status Flow

Locked → To Do → In Progress → Pending Approval → Completed

How to Approve a Task

Step-by-step approval flow

1
Find the taskTasks in Pending Approval appear at the top of your My Tasks tab, highlighted.
2
Open and reviewClick the task to expand it. Read all fields, notes, and any attached documents.
3
Make your decisionClick ✓ Approve to complete the task, or ✕ Reject to send it back.
4
If rejecting — add a commentA comment field appears. Explain clearly what needs to be corrected. This comment is emailed to the assignee.
5
SubmitThe task either completes (approve) or resets to To Do with your comment (reject).
Approvals unblock the entire project chainA task stuck in Pending Approval stops all downstream tasks from unlocking. Review approval tasks daily to avoid project delays.

Your Own Tasks

In addition to approvals, some tasks are directly assigned to you — for example, scheduling delivery, creating Gate Passes, or completing F2 Production List. Work these tasks the same way: set to In Progress, complete the action, mark as Completed.

Timesheets — Daily Entry

Go to the Timesheets tab → Log Entry sub-view to record a worker's hours for the day.

Section 5

Project Workflow — Your Slice

The Manager role is a gatekeeper at multiple critical checkpoints. Here is where you appear in each phase.

Phase 1: Preparing

  • Monitor SED activity — if any project has no action for 3 days you are notified automatically
  • Receive notification when Phase 2 begins (client approved the quote)

Phase 2: Open

  • Receive notification when project advances from Preparing → Open
  • Review F5 quotation items submitted by SED
  • Approve per-item tasks that require manager sign-off

Phase 3: Working — Material & Fabrication

  • Create Gate Pass once fabrication is complete and items are ready
  • Inform the client of the estimated supply/delivery date
  • Check site readiness, then confirm the exact delivery date with the client
  • Notify the accountant of expected payment timing
  • F2 Production List — enter the production timeline (start + end dates) on the relevant task
  • Assign Installation Team — select team members for the project
  • Send notification to the full team once installation team is assigned

Phase 3: Working — Installation

  • Schedule the delivery date
  • Notify client to make the delivery payment; notify the accountant
  • Approval to complete fixing — review installation team notes and approve to proceed

Phase 4: Closing

  • Notify client to pay the final payment; notify accountant
  • Sign off on F6 Handover Sheet
  • Confirm project is moved to Closed status
Section 6

Common Scenarios

Scenario 1 — I have several Pending Approval tasks. How do I process them?

1
Open My Tasks. Pending Approval tasks appear at the top.
2
Work through them one by one — expand each task, review the details.
3
For each: Approve (green button) if correct, or Reject (red button) with a written comment explaining what to fix.
4
Approved tasks complete immediately; rejected tasks return to the assignee with your comment via email.

Scenario 2 — Fabrication is done. How do I issue a Gate Pass and schedule delivery?

1
You receive a system notification that Fabrication Done is complete.
2
Open the project in the Projects tab.
3
Click Gate Pass. Fill in the items description and estimated supply date. Submit.
4
Call the client to confirm site readiness and give the exact delivery date. Update the Gate Pass with the confirmed delivery date.
5
Assign the installation team via the Install Teams tab if not already done.

Scenario 3 — I need to log a worker's hours for today

1
Go to Timesheets tab → Log Entry.
2
Select the date (defaults to today), worker, and project.
3
Enter regular and overtime hours. The total is shown live.
4
Click Log Entry. The entry appears in Today's Entries below the form.

Scenario 4 — I need to review the weekly worker hours

1
Go to Timesheets tab → Weekly Summary.
2
Use the Prev / Next navigation to select the week.
3
Expand each worker card to see their daily breakdown, total regular, total overtime, and grand total hours.

Scenario 5 — An installation team member is not in the dropdown

Contact the Superadmin — they manage the installation team member list in the Users section. Once a user is added and activated with the Installation role, they will appear in the assignment dropdown.

Section 7

Permissions Reference

What you CAN do

  • Approve or reject any task in Pending Approval status
  • Create Gate Passes on any project
  • Assign installation team members to projects
  • Schedule and confirm delivery dates
  • Add and view payment records
  • Submit F5, F3, and F6 forms
  • Log and edit worker timesheet entries
  • View all projects across all stages
  • Add manager notes on any project
  • View and use the payment calendar

What you CANNOT do

  • Advance a project stage manually (Superadmin only)
  • Set the "Call the Client" outcome (Superadmin only)
  • Create or manage user accounts
  • Access the superadmin dashboard, reports, or KPI section
  • Delete timesheet entries (Superadmin only)
  • Manage the worker roster (add/remove workers — Superadmin only)

Section 8

Troubleshooting

ProblemLikely CauseSolution
A task is in Pending Approval but I cannot see approve/reject buttonsThe task may require a different approver, or you may be viewing it in read-only modeEnsure you are logged in as Manager. Reload the page. If still missing, contact the Superadmin.
The installation team dropdown is empty when assigningNo users with the Installation role are active in the systemAsk the Superadmin to add or activate installation team members in the Users section.
A worker is not in the timesheet dropdownThe worker has not been added to the Workers roster, or is marked InactiveContact the Superadmin to add or activate the worker in the Workers management page.
Timesheet entry rejected — duplicate errorAn entry already exists for that worker + project + dateFind the existing entry in Today's Entries panel and edit it rather than creating a new one.
Gate Pass button is not available on a projectThe project may not be in Working stage, or fabrication is not yet completeCheck the project stage and confirm that the Fabrication Done task is Completed.
Payment calendar shows wrong datesPayment due dates were not entered when creating payment recordsOpen the payment record and add the Due Date field.
A project is stuck with no new tasks unlockingA prerequisite task is Pending Approval or In Progress untouchedCheck all tasks in the project chain for any that need approval or action.
